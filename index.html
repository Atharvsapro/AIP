The Products We get From Forests

Wood


Wood is an essential product in our lives
We use wood for many purposes such as:
1. Furniture – Many of our furniture such as a few chairs , tables , closets or almirahs etc.
2. Stationary – Paper and pencil which play a major role in our lives are obtained by wood
3. Instruments – A number of instruments like Piano, violin, cello, drums, flute, guitar, bass etc. are built by wood
4. Wooden Toys - Cricket, hockey, billiard, table tennis etc. Toys and sports
equipment have long made use of wood for handles and main parts.

Medicines


Quite a few medicines are obtained from forests
1 Reserpine- It is used for the treatment of high blood pressure and is obtained from roots of certain species of the tropical plant Rauwolfia.
2 Quinine: It is used for treatment of malaria and is obtained from the bark of cinchona tree
3 Ipecac: It is given immediately after a case of poison It is obtained from the dried rhizome and roots of the ipecacuanha plant
4 Ephedrine: It is used to prevent low blood pressure during spinal anesthesia. Forest products are also effective in the treatment of cancer.

Oxygen


Forests are called the lungs of the earth because Forests are called lungs of the earth because
they absorb an enormous amount of carbon dioxide that circulate in the atmosphere
and release fresh oxygen in the earth .

Honey


Honey is obtained from bees and is stored in a hive ,
About 70% of the hives are present in forests
1 Honey has a sweet taste and is healthier than sugar
2 It helps improve cholesterol
3 It can also Lower Triglycerides

Rubber


1 Rubber is extracted by method called tapping, by making incisions into the bark and collecting the fluid into vessels attached to the rubber trees. The liquid is a sticky, milky sap called latex
2 Once the latex is collected from the plantation, is rushed to the processing plant
3 The latex is heated and mixed with acid ammonia which forms rubber like curds.
4 The sheets of natural rubber that are formed are cut and stacked ready for shipment to rubber molders
